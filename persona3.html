Robert Chen

🔬 Congenital Anosmia

📋 Basic Information

Age 28
Occupation Software Engineer
Location San Francisco, CA
Duration Lifetime (28 yrs)
Tech Comfort Very High

🩺 Symptoms

Congenital Anosmia
Note: Complete inability to detect any odors since birth

💪 Motivations

  • Safety monitoring (gas, smoke, spoiled food)
  • Build scent memory through technology
  • Explain his condition to others
  • Connect through scent alternatives

💔 Pain Points

  • Constant anxiety about gas leaks & fire
  • Social awkwardness when others expect him to smell
  • Food disappointment — can't experience complex flavors
  • No scent-related memories to connect with people

🎯 Primary Goals

  • Develop safety strategies
  • Understand the scented world
  • Raise awareness about congenital anosmia
  • Participate in research studies

⚡ Key Feature Usage

Safety Logs Critical
Scent Descriptions High
PDF Export Medium
Country Selection Low
🕐 Daily Usage Pattern
Morning: Logs any unusual experiences
Throughout Day: Logs when others mention smells
Evening: Reviews logs & notes changes
Weekly: Reviews safety logs & patterns
"I've never known what a rose smells like. But I want to understand what I'm missing, and keep myself safe." — Robert
